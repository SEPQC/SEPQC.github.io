Our Manifesto:

-“Higher education is a human right” - Free CUNY Petition to City Council Advocate for student stipends, pay parity for adjuncts, and the elimination of tuition for all
-Give every student a platform for their voice to be heard through horizontal democracy, which replaces hierarchy with collaboration, coordination and shared responsibility for all decisions and outcomes.
-Protect and advocate for Womxn, LGBTQIA+, People of Color, Black, Muslim, Jewish, Latinx, Brown, immigrant, disabled, and other disadvantaged students on campus
-Build an empowered, intersectional community of opportunity
-Improve Campus Communication and Transparency
-Enhance Sustainability and Infrastructure on Campus
-Promote activism on campus and beyond while encouraging participatory discussion and action.
-Prepare, Educate, and Encourage all students in their initiatives and activities.
